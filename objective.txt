Stage |                         Problems (Assumptions)                          |                                                                                                 Possible Solutions (Tactics)                                                                                                  |
+-------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|     1 | Users unable to play: Not understanding game concepts, controls, format | Game and Format Education: Demo games, How to play Videos, Simple in-game controls and format onboarding, UX hacks like prompting users to 'rules sheet' when they seem stuck, Detailed game LMS (Learning Management System) |
|  1, 2 | Users unable to play: Not finding opponents quickly                     | Human like bots - ensuring no user money is lost, matchmaking relaxations and 'teleporting' free users to waiting tables, increasing liquidity with additional performance marketing                                          |
|     2 | Users unable to play:Not finding cheap tables, money getting in way    | Price tiers personalised by rounds played, giving enough play money to cross stage 1, 2                                                                                                                                       |
|  2, 3 | Users don't want to play - games are boring                             | Constant experiments/improvements in game design, Personalising games available and creating progressions, Cross promotions                                                                                                   |
|  2, 3 | Users don't want to play - don't have money to play (real money averse) | Ignore these users, feedback to user acquisition team to find better users                                                                                                                                                    |
|     3 | Users don't want to play - not winning enough                           | Rake back programs, High loss ratio alerts to matchmaking logic, Leaderboards                                                                                                                                                 |
|     3 | Users playing other games too much (cannibalisation)                    | Schedule experiments to tweak availability of games in the direction Trivia -> Casual -> Cards                                                                                                                                |
